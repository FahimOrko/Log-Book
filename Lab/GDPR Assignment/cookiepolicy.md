This policy explains how our online booking platform uses cookies and similar technologies.

## Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device to enhance your experience, save preferences, and manage session information.

## Types of Cookies We Use

- **Session Cookies:** Maintain login sessions and protect against CSRF attacks.
- **Functional Cookies:** Ensure booking forms and other platform features work correctly.

## Managing Cookies

You can block or delete cookies via your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features.

## Compliance with GDPR

- Cookies do not store sensitive personal data such as passwords or birthdates.
- By using the platform, users implicitly consent to cookies.
- Users can withdraw consent at any time by clearing cookies or adjusting browser settings.
