## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we gather, use, and safeguard your personal information when using our booking platform. We adhere to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## Information We Collect

The types of personal data collected include:

- Email address (used as your login identifier)
- Date of birth (to verify users are over 15)
- User role (reserver or administrator)
- Session and CSRF tokens
- Login records, including IP addresses
- Booking details (start and end times of reservations)

## Purpose of Data Use

Your information is processed for:

- Creating and managing user accounts
- Handling reservations and managing bookings
- Performing administrative tasks (accessible only to authorized admins)
- Enforcing age restrictions for reservations

## Data Minimization

We only collect the information necessary to operate and provide the booking service effectively.

## User Rights

Under GDPR, users are entitled to:

- Access the personal data we hold about them
- Request deletion of their personal data
- Withdraw consent for processing
- Correct any inaccuracies in their data

## Data Protection

- Passwords are securely hashed using bcrypt.
- Protections against CSRF, XSS, and SQL injection are in place.
- Logs and session information are maintained without exposing sensitive personal details.

## Data Sharing

We do not disclose your personal data to third parties unless legally required.
